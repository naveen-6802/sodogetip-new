Frequently asked questions(FAQs) for/on SoDogeTip

The term “on-chain” means that tips are directly processed on the official Dogecoin blockchain which improves traceability and security compared to an off-chain solution.

Tips are saved and you will receive any pending tips a few hours after you registered.

Tips that go to non-registered users are pending for three days, after that time the tipped funds go back to the tipper if the tippee hasn’t registered an account during that time.

We can use emails to send validation links and codes for withdrawal. Also, you may receive emails with important information. But rest assured, there won’t be any ads sent via email.

The tipping bot can’t access that email address for security reasons, so you need to register an email address which the bot can use to send you emails.

There are no minimum amount to withdraw, but you need to pay mining fee
